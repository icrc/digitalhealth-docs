# Roles & Tasks

{% hint style="warning" %}
These roles reflect the **default access settings** in Odoo, but depending on the context or organization setup, the actual responsibilities may differ in practice. Always refer to your local workflow for accurate role assignments.
{% endhint %}

***

### P\&O (Prosthetist & Orthotist)

**Access:** Service User records, Material Requests, and Repair functions.

> You manage the full device lifecycle — from the initial request to the final delivery to the Service User.

[👉 See all P\&O tasks](p-and-o-prosthetist-and-orthotist.md)

***

### Benchworker

**Access:** Manufacturing and Repair modules, with limited access to Stock.

> You carry out the physical manufacturing steps in the workshop.

[👉 See all Benchworker tasks](benchworker.md)

***

### Stock Manager (Storekeeper)

**Access:** Stock, Manufacturing, and Repair modules.

> You handle all stock movements and ensure components are available and allocated before production begins.

[👉 See all Stock Manager tasks](stock-manager-storekeeper.md)

***

### Head of P\&O / Supervisor

**Access:** Same as P\&O, with additional rights to validate Manufacturing Orders and authorize certain steps in the manufacturing process.

> You validate and approve key steps to ensure the manufacturing process follows the correct workflow.

👉[ See all Head of P\&O tasks](head-of-p-and-o-supervisor.md)

***

### DCMS Administrator

**Access:** Center Administrator with broad administrative access.

> You manage the system configuration and ensure the platform is correctly set up for all other roles to work efficiently.

👉 [See all DCMS Administrator tasks](dcms-administrator.md)

***

### Other Roles

The following roles exist in the system but are either not yet active or have limited scope:

* **Dormitory Manager** — Access limited to Dormitory management information
* **Receptionist** — Role defined but not currently in use
* **Social Worker** — Role defined but not currently in use
* **Admin L3** — ICRC Administrator with full system access
