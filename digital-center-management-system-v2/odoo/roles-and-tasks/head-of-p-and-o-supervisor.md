# Head of P\&O / Supervisor

> You validate and approve key steps to ensure the manufacturing process follows the correct workflow.

{% hint style="info" %}
Roles in Odoo: **Head of P\&O**. See Roles and Privileges for full access details.
{% endhint %}

### Your Tasks

#### Approvals & Validation

1. Validate the BoM Configuration — review and confirm before MRP creation
2. Confirm the MRP Order — requires the `group_mrp_manager` role
3. Approve Greenlight work order steps — only your role can unblock these steps
4. Validate the final Manufacturing Order — mark as Done once all steps are complete
