# Receptionist

## **Receptionist**



**1.** When you finished with the HSU basic service plan, **send patient to the receptionist**.

**2**. Open **‘Add Clinical Consent’.**

**3.** Select either digital or paper **consent form.**

**4.** Make **HSU sign** consent form.
