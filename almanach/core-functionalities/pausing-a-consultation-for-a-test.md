# Pausing a consultation for a test

Sometimes you will have to pause a consultation to perform a test on a beneficiary. You can pause the consultation for

* A malaria rapid diagnostic test
* A urine dipstick test
* A bronchodilator trial
* A repeat temperature measurement
* A repeat respiratory rate measurement

### Pausing a consultation&#x20;

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/shared image (22).jfif" alt="" width="188"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

If you accept to pause your consultation you will be prompted to enter a text to identify the consultation when you resume the consultation after the test is performed.&#x20;

When prompted please enter a unique identifier a that will help you to identify the consultation later.&#x20;

When you enter a comment and submit the form, you will end the consultation and you will have to resume the consultation as outlined in the section below.&#x20;

### Resuming a consultation after pause

You can resume a consultation by navigating to the **Tasks** tab in the CHT application.

When you navigate to the **Tasks** tab you will see the available consultations for you to resume.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (8).jfif" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Select the consultation that you want to resume, and continue the consultation until the end and submit the consultation.&#x20;
